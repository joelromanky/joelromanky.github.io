Bio

Hello 👋,

I am a PhD Candidate in Computer Science from University of Lorraine and an Engineer 🎓 graduated from Toulouse INP-ENSEEIHT in Computer Science and Telecommunications 💻.
I did my preparatory classes at CPGE Mohamed V in Casablanca, Morocco. I went to a special high school, a military one 👮‍♂️, the Prytanée Militaire de Kadiogo. I got my high school diploma there as well as military training certificates.

My PhD activities are about classification and troubleshooting of Cloud Gaming applications and on low latency applications more broadly. They are conducted within the research team RESIST in the LORIA lab and within Orange Innovation through a CIFRE contract.

My work interests are Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Statistics. I am strongly interested in Research and Development in these fields.
Beyond work, I’m a huge fan of Japanese manga 👹 and piano 🎹. I also like volley-ball and e-gaming 🎮.
